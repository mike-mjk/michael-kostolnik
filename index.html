Jiu-Jitsu Tube

A video based social network for members of my jiu-jitsu gym to cut through the clutter of YouTube jiu-jitsu videos to share only the best content. It is also now the centralized location for my members of my gym to share their competition videos.

Stargazing Weather

This simple app allows users to quickly find out which of the next ten nights will be good for stargazing based on how much cloud cover there will be. It also shows users if the International Space Station will be visible from their location.
